Strawberry Cream Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of crushed graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 2 cups of fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup of cream cheese
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the crushed graham crackers and melted butter until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust.
  4. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  5. Add the vanilla extract and mix until well incorporated.
  6. Gently fold in the fresh strawberries.
  7. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is set.
  9. Let the cheesecake cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
