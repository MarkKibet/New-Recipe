Blackforest Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup cherries, pitted and halved
  • Chocolate shavings for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to the butter mixture, alternating with buttermilk. Mix until just combined. Stir in hot water.
  5. Divide batter evenly between the prepared pans. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool cakes in pans for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
  7. In a bowl, whip heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  8. Place one cake layer on a serving plate. Spread with whipped cream and top with cherries. Place the second cake layer on top and spread with more whipped cream. Garnish with chocolate shavings and additional cherries.
