Strawberry Cream Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 packages (8 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, 3 tablespoons sugar, and melted butter. Press into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese and 1/2 cup sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
  4. Pour cream cheese mixture over crust. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until center is set.
  5. Mix sour cream, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract. Spread over cheesecake. Bake for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Cool cheesecake on a wire rack. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Top with fresh strawberries before serving.
